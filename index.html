Your Role on a Call

A call is often the fastest and most reassuring way to resolve a client's concern. Every call is an opportunity to build trust. Be calm, speak clearly, and always follow up on chat after the call the written record matters as much as the conversation itself.

⚠️

Ground Rules Non-Negotiable

Must follow at all times

These rules apply before, during, and after every call. There are absolutely no exceptions to these.

🚫
Never call from your personal number. Always place calls only via Exotel, under any circumstances.
🔒
Never share your personal mobile number with any client, even if they directly ask for it.
🔍
Always check Finder / previous chats before calling the client. This gives you better context before the conversation starts.
🚫
Never use the words: advisory / suggestion / recommendation in any part of the conversation.
💬
Mention on chat that you are initiating a call, before you actually call. Always keep the chat updated.
📋
After every call, provide closure on chat confirm resolution and include a brief summary of what was discussed.

Disconnecting the Call Important Rule

✅ Default Rule
We will not disconnect the call from our end. Always wait for the client to disconnect first.
⚠ Exception When You May Disconnect
You may inform the client and end the call from our side only when both of the following conditions are true:

1. The client has kept the call on hold for an extended period of time, AND
2. The client does not respond at the time of closure, but was actively engaged during the call earlier.

In such cases, please clearly mention this on chat while providing call closure.

How to Make Calls Using Exotel

Always use Exotel never personal number

Follow these steps every time you need to initiate a call with a client. Exotel routes the call securely so that neither you nor the client's personal number is directly revealed.

1
Click the CALL button on the top-left corner of the Exotel dashboard. It is the blue "Call" button. You will find it on the top navigation bar when you are logged in to Exotel.
2
Under 'Make a call'Number box option, enter the client's phone number and click Call. Type the number carefully. Double-check it before clicking Call.
📸 Exotel Dashboard Making a Call
Click the CALL button (top-left), enter the client's number, then click CALL to connect.
3
The first ring will come to your number (the IR your registered number). This is normal. Pick up your phone when it rings.
4
Once you answer, Exotel will automatically connect the call to the client. This ensures the call is routed securely neither your number nor the client's number is directly revealed to the other party.

How to Send Messages via Exotel SMS

📸 Exotel Dashboard Sending an SMS
Click the SMS button (top-left), enter the number, type or add from template, then click SEND.
1
Click on Messages in the Exotel dashboard.
2
Enter the client's mobile number in the "To" field.
3
Select the relevant approved template and click Send. Always use the appropriate template do not type a free-form message unless instructed.
ℹ Available SMS Templates on Exotel
Templates include: call not connected message, feedback call message, and WhatsApp redirection message. Always select the most relevant one based on the situation.

When and How to Go on a Call Case by Case

Use your judgement to decide when a call is appropriate. Do not call every investor by default. Below are the three standard cases to guide your decision.

Case 1

📋 Investor requests a call but has NOT shared any questions

First, request the investor to share their queries and preferred time. This helps you prepare and connect them to the right person.

Suggested Response

We request you to please let us know the reason for the call so that I can connect you with the concerned team or person. Kindly also share your preferred time and language for the call.

⚠ If No Response
If the investor does not reply for 20–30 minutes, you may proceed to call them directly. Ensure you call at the confirmed time. If there is a delay, inform the client in advance.
Case 2

💬 Investor has shared multiple questions a call would be more efficient

You may suggest a call if it helps resolve queries faster and more clearly. Always ask for the investor's consent before calling. Make sure they are comfortable with a call.

💡 Important
Use this approach with good judgement avoid calling every investor by default. If the user is non-responsive for 20–30 minutes, proceed to answer their queries over chat instead.
Case 3

🤔 Investor is not able to clearly understand the responses on chat

If written communication is not working, ask the investor if you may connect over a call for better clarity. If they do not respond for 20–30 minutes, proceed to call. If the call is not answered, try to explain the query over chat using reference links or screenshots instead.

Mandatory Call Scenarios Must Not Be Missed

No exceptions allowed

For these specific situations, you must get on a call with the user without exception. These are high-priority and cannot be handled over chat alone.

Must-Call 1

💰 Repayment Issues

Any concern regarding repayment must be addressed over a call. This ensures clarity and prevents further confusion or escalation.

Must-Call 2

💳 Fund-Related Issues

Issues involving funds such as delays or discrepancies are sensitive and require immediate attention via a direct call.

Must-Call 3

📈 Escalated Issues

When a case has already been escalated, prioritise the user with direct communication to resolve their concern promptly.

Must-Call 4

😤 Irate / Frustrated User

A call can de-escalate anger and frustration far more effectively than chat. It shows empathy and helps reach faster resolution.

Other Situations Where a Call is Strongly Recommended

  • 📋Complicated or urgent queries that require detailed explanation or immediate resolution to avoid unnecessary back-and-forth on chat.
  • 🗓Day-long unresolved queries if an inquiry has been going on for an entire day without a clear resolution, escalate it to a call.

Guidelines During the Call

Follow these guidelines throughout every call from the moment the client picks up to the moment you say goodbye.

  • 1
    Begin with a proper greeting and introduction.
    Sample Opening

    Good morning / afternoon, this is [Your Name] calling from Wint Wealth. We received your request for a call regarding your query. Please let me know how I may assist you.

  • 2
    Address the client's query clearly after completing your introduction. Do not jump straight into the query without a proper greeting first.
  • 3
    Speak confidently and empathetically throughout the conversation. Your tone should reassure the client at all times especially if they are worried or confused.
  • 4
    Not sure about the answer? Do not guess. Politely ask the client for some time, verify internally, and call them back with accurate information.
    What to Say on the Call

    Sir, I will check this with my team and get back to you shortly with the correct information.

  • 5
    Keep the client's account details ready on Finder before initiating the call. Never go into a call unprepared.
  • 6
    Use simple language avoid technical jargon. Explain things in a way that any user can understand, regardless of their background.
  • 7
    Remain calm and composed at all times even if the client is agitated or upset. Your tone must always be reassuring and professional. Never match the client's frustration.
  • 8
    After addressing the main query, ask if there are any further questions before wrapping up. Never assume the call is over without confirming this.
  • 9
    Close the call with clear expectations.
    If Query is Resolved

    ✅ Resolved Closure

    Sample

    It was great assisting you today. Have a great day!

    If Further Checks are Needed

    ⏳ Pending Closure

    Sample

    Please allow me some time to check the details with the team. I will call you back shortly with an update.

    🚨 Always Do This After the Call
    After disconnecting, update the chat immediately. Mention what was discussed, the outcome, and the next steps (if any). This is mandatory not optional.

Chat Closure After Every Call

Once the call is completed, you must always close the loop on chat. The chat record is as important as the call itself.

1
Confirm resolution or next steps on chat
Standard Post-Call Message

Hope I was able to resolve all your queries on the call. Please feel free to reach out if you need any further assistance. Have a great day!

2
Add a brief summary of what was discussed Do not close the chat without a short summary. For example: mention the topic discussed, what was resolved, and any follow-up action if applicable.
3
If call was not answered send a message and wait
Call Not Answered Chat Message

Sir, as per your request I called you. Unfortunately, the call was not answered. Please let me know your next preferred time to call you back.

Wait 30 minutes. If no response → Close as No Response.
4
Apply the correct closure tag and close the chat
Resolved Calls_Directly Calls_Video Calls_Onboarding No Response
💡 Advisory Query Handling on Call
If the client asks whether Wint Wealth provides portfolio management or investment advisory services, use this response:

"We are a bond investment platform where we partner with various NBFCs and list their bonds. We are not a SEBI registered advisor and do not offer services such as investment advisory or portfolio management."

Chat Tagging Guidelines for Call Chats

Mandatory for tracking

Accurate tagging is mandatory for tracking and reporting. Always apply the correct tag before closing the chat after a call.

  • 🎥
    Calls_Video
    Use when you have gone on a Google Meet video call with the user.
    Calls_Video
  • 📞
    Calls_Directly
    Use when you have gone on a call with the user immediately / directly without any waiting or scheduling.
    Calls_Directly
  • 🎓
    Calls_Onboarding
    Use when the user asked about platform basics, bonds, how to invest, or KYC details on the call.
    Calls_Onboarding
⚠ Reminder
Incorrect tagging directly affects team tracking and reporting. If you are unsure which tag to use, check with your TL before closing the chat.

A Note to All Associates

Every call is a chance to turn a worried investor into a confident one. Be prepared before you dial. Be patient during the call. And always close the loop on chat. Timely and clear communication is the key to ensuring user satisfaction and minimising further issues.
